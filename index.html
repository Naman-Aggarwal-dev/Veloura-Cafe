Our Story

Crafted with Passion Since 2008

Veloura Café was born from a simple desire: to create a sanctuary where the aroma of freshly ground beans meets the warmth of genuine hospitality. We source our beans directly from sustainable farms in Colombia and Ethiopia.

Every latte, every pastry, every cup is a testament to our commitment to quality. Step inside, let the world slow down, and let your cup tell you a story.
